Description

Priprema: 1. Krumpir operite, stavite ga cijelog u veći lonac, zalijte vodom, dodajte žličicu soli pa ga kuhajte oko 45-50 minuta, odnosno dok krumpir ne bude kuhan (probajte čačkalicom). Krumpir ocijedite, ogulite i protisnite kroz prešu za krumpir pa mu odmah, dok je još vruć, dodajte na kockice rezani maslac i napravite pire. Ostavite ga da se potpuno ohladi.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg krumpira
  • 300 g pšeničnog glatkog brašna
  • 100 g maslaca
  • 1 jaje
  • 1 žličica soli

Steps:

  1. Krumpir operite, stavite ga cijelog u veći lonac
  2. U ohlađeni krumpir dodajte jedno jaje i brašno
  3. U manjoj zdjelici izmiješajte šećer s mljevenim cimetom
  4. U dubljem loncu zakuhajte vodu, dodajte prstohvat soli i žlicu ulja pa ubacite knedle
  5. U dubljoj tavi zagrijte ulje, dodajte krušne mrvice pa ih pržite do zlatnosmeđe boje

The way you blend the ingredients is crucial: under-beating can lead to a lumpy mixture, over-beating can whip in too much air. This can result in uneven cooking, bubbles, and cracking.

Ingredients:

  • 85g butter, plus extra for tin
  • 140g digestive biscuits
  • 1 tbsp sugar, granulated or golden caster
  • 250g golden caster sugar

Steps:

  1. Position an oven shelf in the middle of the oven. Heat the oven to 180C/ 160C fan/ gas 4.
  2. Line the base of a 23cm springform cake tin by putting a square piece
  3. For the crust, melt 85g butter in a medium pan. Stir in 140g digestive biscuit crumbs
